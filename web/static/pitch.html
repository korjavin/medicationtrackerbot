From Fragmented Data to Personal Health Intelligence

How we turned a mess of health apps into a single, private, and intelligent companion.

The Challenge

Health Data Was Everywhere. And Nowhere.

We started with a simple frustration: keeping track of medications, blood pressure, weight, and sleep required four different apps.

None of them talked to each other. None of them gave the full picture. We needed a single source of truth.

Step 1: The Hub

Unifying the Experience

We built a unified hub. A single interface—available on Web and Telegram—that adapts to the user.

It's not just a logbook; it's a proactive assistant. It knows your schedule, reminds you gently, and tracks everything from pill dosages to daily weight trends.

  • Unified Timeline
  • Instant Telegram Logging
  • Smarter Reminders
Step 2: Second Memory

We Remember, So You Don't Have To

Forget the mental load. We send timely notifications for medications, workouts, and missed measurements (like Blood Pressure).

But don't worry—it's a helper, not a dictator. You're always in control: snooze, skip, or confirm with a single tap.

  • Smart Contextual Alerts
  • Snooze & Skip Control
  • Auto-Restock Reminders
Step 3: Beyond Telegram

Web Push Notifications

Love to get notifications but not a Telegram user? Don't worry—Web Push is built right in.

It's platform-agnostic, works on desktop and mobile browsers, and gives you the same proactive health reminders without needing a chat app.

  • Browser Native Alerts
  • Platform-Agnostic
  • Configurable & Private
Step 4: Your Interaction Style

Minimalism or Power? Have Both.

Love data? The Web App offers rich visualizations and works Local-First—log your data even without an internet connection.

Prefer simplicity? The Chat Interface gives you convenient buttons and commands for a distraction-free experience.

  • Rich Web Dashboard
  • Offline-Ready (Local First)
  • Fast Chat Actions
Step 5: Bring Your Own Data

Keep Your Favorite Tools

Have other tracking apps you love? No problem. We believe in an open ecosystem.

Devices like Mi Band are just one example. Our open import system allows for unlimited custom integrations. Just export your data, send it to the bot, and we handle the rest.

  • Mi Band Integration (Example)
  • Unlimited Custom Imports
  • Unified History
Step 6: True Ownership

Private by Design

It's all your data, on your infrastructure. A single database that you, and only you, own.

Worried about backups? Use optional capabilities to stream incremental changes to any S3-compatible cloud.

No vendor lock. Your data is not cryptic. You own it, build any integrations you want.

  • Self-Hosted Foundation
  • Optional Cloud Streaming
  • Zero Vendor Lock-in
Step 7: The Magic Layer

Conversational Intelligence

This is where it gets exciting. We added an "Intelligence Layer" using something called MCP (Model Context Protocol).

Think of it as giving your AI assistant a pair of glasses to read your health diary.

You can now ask plain English questions:

"My blood pressure is higher this week. Does it overlap with my new medication schedule?"
Step 8: Future Plans

Bring Your Own AI

Want constant monitoring from your personal AI? Whether it's self-hosted or your favorite cloud model, we believe in choice.

Bring Your Own Keys (BYOK). Configure your own AI URL and keys, set your trust guardrails (control exactly what data is accessible), and use it for any automation or insight you can dream of.

  • Custom AI Configuration
  • Strict Access Guardrails
  • Unlimited Automation
